Keep your plants healthy with real-time insights from Arteco soil sensors for Homey. The app supports the Zigbee `ZS-301Z` and `ZS-304Z`, measuring soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and light level directly at the root zone, with soil fertility support on `ZS-301Z`.

Set up automatic alerts when the soil gets too dry, fine-tune readings with built-in calibration offsets, and adjust how often the sensor samples. All data is stored as history in Homey so you can spot trends and understand exactly what your plants need.
